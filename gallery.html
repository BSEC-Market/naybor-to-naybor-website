Our Work

Before & After Transformations

Deck Restoration

What we did: Stripped old stain, repaired damaged boards, sanded surfaces, and applied premium exterior stain for a weather-resistant finish.
Materials: Premium wood stain, pressure-treated lumber, deck screws.
Challenges: Significant wood rot required unexpected board replacements and precise color matching for seamless results.

Bathroom Tub Refinishing

What we did: Cleaned, sanded, and refinished a stained bathtub to restore its glossy white finish and extend its lifespan.
Materials: Tub resurfacing kit, bonding agents, waterproof epoxy coating.
Challenges: Addressing deep stains and chips while ensuring a smooth, flawless finish without bubbles or drips.

Garage Organization

What we did: Sorted and decluttered items, installed new shelving, labeled storage bins, and created clear pathways for accessibility.
Materials: Steel shelving, plastic storage totes, labeling system.
Challenges: Heavy clutter required systematic categorizing and creating solutions for awkwardly shaped items.
